Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how this application handles user information and permissions. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Use of Camera

This application requires access to the device camera only to perform face recognition features within the app.
Camera input is used in real time and is not recorded, stored, or saved in any form.
The camera data never leaves the user’s device and is not shared with any external service or third party.

Information Collection

This application does not collect, store, or upload any personal or sensitive user data to external servers.
All processing related to face recognition is performed locally on the user’s device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third party for any purpose.

User Permissions & Control

Camera permission is optional and fully controlled by the user.
Users can grant or revoke camera access at any time through their device’s system settings.

Data Security

We follow standard security practices to ensure that app functionality does not expose user data to unauthorized access.
